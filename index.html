Services

As your librarian, I am available to collaborate on research workshops for your class. These include:

  • Conducting research for a literature review.
  • Analyzing media bias.
  • Using the Children's and YA Literature Collection.
  • Working with primary sources.
  • Finding and analyzing statistics from city, state, and federal education databases.
  • Building GIS projects.

Faculty Research

OER Fellowship

QCL is pleased to invite applications for the Spring 2023 cohort of the Open Educational Resources Fellowship. To date, more than 100 QC faculty from 33 departments have participated. Through this program, Fellows will learn the fundamental principles of open pedagogy and develop technical skills to create and curate accessible course content. Fellows receive a $2500 stipend.

Apply by January 19. Read the CFP.

CUNY Academic Works

Consider uploading your book chapters and articles to Academic Works, CUNY Libraries' free, online repository that provides universal access to the scholarship and creative work of faculty and students. With over 3M annual downloads, Academic Works is a great way to get your publications noticed.

Speak with Nancy Foasberg, Scholarly Communication Librarian, for more info.
